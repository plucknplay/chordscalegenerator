Preferences: Chord Generation View

You can move some of the restrictions for the Chord Generation View in an Advanced Section.

It is advisable to hide less frequent or complicated points in the Advanced Section. But you can also hide restrictions which have a good default value and normally won't have to be changed later on.

The selected restrictions will be placed in the collapsable Advanced Section whereas all the other restrictions will be placed above the Advanced Section where they are permanently visible. If you didn't put anything in the Advanced Section, it will not be shown.
