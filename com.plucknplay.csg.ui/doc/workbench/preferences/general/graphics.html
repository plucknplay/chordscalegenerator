Preferences: Graphics

Regarding the graphics of the application you can set the following preferences:

Anti-Aliasing

Here you can specify whether or not anti-aliasing shall be applied to text- and non-text-elements (meaning circles, ellipses, etc.) of graphical views (Fretboard, Keyboard, Box, Tab und Notes). The default value corresponds to the default behaviour of your operating system.

Interpolation (of Images)

Here you can specify whether or not interpolation shall be applied to images of graphical views. Images are used, for example, in the Notes View to display clefs or notes. The default value corresponds to the default behaviour of your operating system.

Note: Anti-Aliasing as well as interpolation can cause delays in drawing contents of graphical views on weaker computers.
